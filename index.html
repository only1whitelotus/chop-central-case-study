⚠️ The Challenge vs. The Solution

This section illustrates the operational hurdles faced by the restaurant prior to the ERMS implementation. It contrasts the chaotic legacy processes directly against the structured, automated solutions provided by the new system.

Before: The Problems

  • ❌

    Unstructured Ordering

    Students ordered directly from vendors, creating chaotic queues and missed orders.

  • 💸

    Revenue Leakage

    No reliable way to match inventory dispensed with cash collected (shrinkage).

  • ⏳

    Kitchen Bottlenecks

    Mixing ready-made items with made-to-order items caused massive delays.

  • 🕳️

    Data Blindspot

    Management lacked real-time insight into revenue, peak hours, and payment splits.

After: ERMS Solutions

  • ✅

    Centralized POS

    A unified "Pay-First, Ticket-Second" architecture organizes all incoming requests.

  • 🔒

    Strict Accountability

    Staff only dispense food upon presentation of a digitally logged, printed receipt.

  • 🚀

    Intelligent Routing

    The system splits orders, sending only prep-required items to the digital kitchen screen.

  • 📊

    Live Analytics

    Granular, real-time dashboards track every metric, empowering management decisions.

Point of Sale (POS) & Register

Designed for high-speed campus rushes, the POS module handles transactions, precise accounting routing, and physical receipt generation while maintaining resilience against network drops.

  • Payment Method Routing Forces cashiers to specify Cash, Bank Transfer, or POS Swipes at checkout, ensuring precise drawer balancing.
  • Offline-First Capabilities If Wi-Fi drops, the app clears the cart, prints locally, and queues data. Upon reconnection, it silently syncs to the cloud and kitchen.
🖥️
Thermal Print Engine
Natively hooks into device spoolers for 80mm receipts with timestamps & order IDs.
